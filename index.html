Mathkids

A simple game designed for kids where you enter the answer to simple math questions. As you get more correct, the range of the numbers increases and thus creating more difficulty. This was done in a single day as a early html5->mac app experiment using appcelerator titanium.

APP STORE

Gtasktic

A Google Tasks client written in HTML5 and packaged for Mac

Gtasktic is the first app I've written during my work with Taskstrike. It is backed by the local->cloud sync engine that is the core technology I've been working on for Taskstrike. It works offline and online, saving change to tasks you make while offline and then syncing them back again. It also implements real-time sync, where an action you make is synced to the cloud in real time and one time sync, where all the data is synced with the cloud at once.

Google Tasks Offline

A rewrite of Gtasktic for the Chrome App Store

Another app written at Taskstrike. This basically took Gtasktic and repackaged it for the chrome app store and made a limited version free for users. It's my most popular app with over 3400 users and counting!

Kervu.com

Kervu is a website creation tool geared towards craigslist users.

Kervu creates a great informational page for people who advertise on craigslist for services. It also creates a html embed in craigslist that tracks how many people opens your craigslist post. It basically combines analytics and page creation for the craigslist service market.

ABOUT ME

At Marcfolio, we carefully consider every stage of development and pay rigorous attention to each detail in order to create deliverables that relate clear, strategic messages tailored to specific channels.

What We Do

An effective design strategy aligns a consistent expression of a brand's visual identity across a broad range of vehicles. Print, interactive, packaging and environmental design all offer distinct opportunities and impose specific limits. Marcfolio Design seeks the best expression for each in support of the larger concept.

The Marcfolio team embraces a broad definition of design employing an intelligent yet practical approach to our craft.

Branded Stories

With expertise in identity, print, packaging, interactivity and environments, the Marcfolio studio crafts initiatives that integrate multiple disciplines. Through our wide-ranging capabilities and extensive reach, we coordinate programs that address the spectrum of a client's needs from a unified perspective. Our work helps facilitate communication between client stakeholders, resulting in adaptable systems that define visual communication across an organization.

Digital Media

When well executed, interactive design seamlessly blends graphic art with technology and psychology. Applying clear visual thinking in the online environment encourages dynamic understanding between a brand and its customers. An elegant execution elevates content to the primary position and provides easy access to information. At Marcfolio Design, we're known for finding original solutions to diverse problems through the most suitable media.

PROFILE

Established in 2005 by Marc Garner, Marcfolio employs a dedicated team of art directors, designers, project managers, brand strategists and writers.

Death Cab for Cutie

2011 August 13

I know I'm a bit late to the whole "Death Cab for Cutie". I've always admired the poster art that's been created for the band (especially the stuff from Jason Munn). But it wasn't until they interviewed front man Ben Gibbard on NPR and said he was married to Zooey Deschanel that I thought I really should see what all the hipe is about (I mean who doesn't love Zooey and if she likes him enough to marry… I know it's strange logic).

I purchased their latest album and it's pretty good. Not every track is amazing, but I'm officially calling the song titled "You Are a Tourist" the song of the summer. I can't get it out of my mind. Now I have years of Death Cab to catch up on.
